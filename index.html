About Coffee Corner

Time is precious and we know it.
Coffee Corner value quality for the time you spend with us: here you can find monorigin coffee, organic bakery and specialty.
Take your time to chill in our coffee shop, alone or in good company. Or rent a table in the coworking room.

Coffee Corner is the result of the combined culinary skills of Gary, an award-winning chef, and an idea of Louise, an author and manager who has lived as a digital nomad for many years.

Coworking room

Cozy designed shared workspace in a spacious room, tailored to suit the need of a quiet space to work and study.
Be creative and productive, while enjoying a fresh cup of coffee and organic bakery or a lunch.
You can reserve your own private desk or a sit at the coworking table by the hour or a day.
Free WiFi and access to the Coffee Shop.
